What We Are

Classic 'Cade
Done Right

Arcade Bar South is Gainesville's go-to destination for old-school stand-up cabinets and vintage pinball machines. Spread across three unique floors, we blend the nostalgia of classic arcade gaming with a full bar experience — the kind of place where the night disappears between high scores and happy hours.

Three Floors · One Epic Night

Explore the Levels

Each floor has its own vibe, its own games, and its own bar. Pick your zone — or conquer all three.

01
Ground Floor

Step in and dive straight into the action. The ground floor wraps a tiki-style bar with a full orbit of shooters, fighters, and classic pinball.

  • Vintage pinball machines
  • Shooter & fighter stand-ups
  • Tiki-style full-service bar
  • Sports TVs throughout
02
Mezzanine

Head up to the mezzanine for a bird's-eye view of the action below, more games, and a great spot to post up with friends.

  • Overlooking main floor views
  • Lounge seating area
  • Bubble hockey table
  • Arcade games throughout
03
Top Floor

The crown of the building. Massive space, the biggest bar, DJ capability, and a full lineup of games — plus private event hosting.

  • Skeeball & foosball
  • Pinballs & multi-game cabinets
  • Large full-service bar + sports TVs
  • DJ-ready sound system
  • Private event hosting available
